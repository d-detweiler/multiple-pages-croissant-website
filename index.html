A croissant is a French pastry known for its crescent shape and flaky texture, made from a laminated yeast dough similar to puff pastry. It is inspired by the Austrian kipferl and has a rich history, with origins tracing back to Central Europe. Croissants are typically enjoyed plain or filled with various ingredients, such as ham and cheese or chocolate. For those interested in making them, there are numerous recipes available that guide you through the process of creating these delicious pastries from scratch.
